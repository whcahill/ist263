Meet the B Team

Each department is lead by a member of our executive board. Get to know who’s in charge of what!

Jaelin Slater

Jaelin Slater is our Executive Producer and oversees the rest of the executive board. She is the one who makes the tough decisions and manages the daily operations of the organization as a whole. She is in the class of 2022 and is studying psychology and forensic science. She’s also a major fan of conspiracy theories and cohosts a show with our Director, Cameron Werbinski, on WERW called, Theoretically, dedicated to dissecting them.

Matthew Stetter

Matthew Stetter is one of our Head Writers. He is in the class of 2022 and he’s studying TV Radio Film. He has more nicknames than we can count and has a talent for controlling a room full of people all looking to get the last word in. He’s also delivered groundbreaking performances in such sketches as Password Recovery and such live characters as Germery. He’s going places and he knows it.

Jeanette Orlando

Jeanette Orlando is one of our Head Writers. She is in the class of 2022 and is studying TV Radio Film. She loves ice skating after meetings and, for that reason, cannot stand the Spring, Summer or Autumn seasons. She has no nicknames, but doesn’t let that get her down. You’ll always find her making an effort to socialize with the rest of the organization and make sure everyonne’s feeling comfortable.

Danny Kahn

Danny Kahn is our Head of Graphics. She is in the class of 2023 and is studying graphic design. She’s extremely dedicated to her major and isn’t afraid to stalk a friend’s art account while silently passing judgement, or loudly granting praise.

Will Cahill

William Cahill is our Head of Content Creation. He is in the class of 2022 and is majoring in Information Management and Tech as well as TV Radio Film. He prefers sad movies to happy ones but doesn’t let that impact his work when making comedy videos (mostly). He is a member of Delta Upsilon and is in the running to be the show’s one and only web developer.

Lucas Gibson

Lucas Gibson is our Head of Social Media. He is in the class of 2023 and is studying advertising. His favorite movie is Ghostbusters and he get beyond excited whenever we produce something ghost related (it makes us suspicious sometimes). He asks that people call him Lucas or Gibby, but adamantly warns all to never call him late for lunch.

Cameron Werbinski

Cameron Werbinski is our Head of Crew and Director. She is also in the class of 2022 and she’s studying TV Radio Film and Marketing. Her favorite musical artists are Harry Styles and The Doors. When she’s not working on the show, she cohosts a show on WERW dedicated to discussing the many facets of conspiracies called, Theoretically, with our EP, Jaelin Slater.
