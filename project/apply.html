Every semester we hire a new group of passionate, hardworking students who are looking to learn more about producing a show and working in a creative and collaborative environment.

Writers

Writers are in charge of pitching and crafting ideas for all our segments into usable scripts. They’re the foundation of the organization and the biggest department.

Click here for the Writers app

Content Creators

Content Creators take scripts from writers and produce 2 pre-taped sketches per episode. They plan, shoot, and edit in small teams to bring writer’s ideas to life.

Click here for the CCers app

Social Media

Socials make our presence known and work to build out our audience. They post promotional material and highlight clips from episodes so more viewers can find us.

Click here for the Socials app

Crew

The Crew operates the studio on show night. Camera operators, sound technicians and control room operators rehearse every step of the program to ensure the live broadcast is flawless.

Click here for the Crew app

Graphics

Graphic Designers provide logos, title cards, and photoshop cutaways to give our segments an extra edge and make our jokes hit harder.

Click here for the Graphics app
