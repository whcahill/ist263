Welcome to Live From Studio B!

OTN's #1 late night comedy show

We’re a student run production club, currently producing our 9th season of interviews, games, sketches, musical numbers, story times, monologues, and more!

Our show was first pitched by Lizzie Michael and Josh Feinblatt in 2016 and since then we’ve grown into a well oiled machine of writers, graphic designers, content creators, crew members, and social media managers. Over 80 students contribute to making a half hour of content every other week and we couldn’t be more proud of what we do.
